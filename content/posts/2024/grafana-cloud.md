title: Using Grafana Cloud Pro
date: 2024-01-02
---

I have using Grafana Cloud Free plan to monitor my Homelab almost 1 month and very satisfied with it. As itself claimed, they are very generous on the Free plan and it is very useful. But the only limit is 14 days data retention of the metrics. So today I updated the plan from Free to Pro, without price increase, still $0/Month, because I don't use enterprise plugin, and I am the only active user.
